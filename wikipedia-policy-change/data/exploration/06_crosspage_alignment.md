## Divergence (the finding)
SG `:3` (activity requirement: ≥100 edits in 12 months), `:5` (eligibility lapses on inactivity), `:6`
(restored after 100 new edits) have **no equivalent in Stemprocedure**. The 2018 vote applied the
activity requirement **only to personnel votes** (moderator appointment/removal, ArbCom
elections/removal, block polls — SG `:7`), *not* to the general Stemprocedure. So **eligibility is
venue-dependent**: general votes use the 2 base conditions; personnel votes add the activity gate.
A page-level or same-name matcher would miss this; the atomic level surfaces it as a real divergence.
